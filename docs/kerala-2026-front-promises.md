# Kerala 2026 Front Promises

This note captures pre-election manifesto promises from the three major Kerala fronts in the 2026 Assembly election. It is intended to serve as working source material for Track Promises, where each promise should later become a separate tracked record.

For this election, the tracking context is:

- Tenant: Kerala
- Timeline: 2026 Assembly election
- Major fronts: United Democratic Front (UDF), Left Democratic Front (LDF), National Democratic Alliance (NDA)
- Source type: manifesto, campaign comparison article, and election page
- Tracking unit: one promise per record

## Why This Matters

Kerala is a clear example of why the product has to support multiple fronts within one election timeline.

- Multiple fronts published overlapping welfare promises.
- Several promises use similar numbers, such as `Rs. 3,000` pensions, but differ on eligibility and delivery mechanism.
- A single election contains a mix of welfare, jobs, health, and infrastructure commitments that should be tracked independently.

## LDF Promise Themes

### Welfare, Poverty, And Jobs

1. Deliver a 60-point programme with 950 proposals for a `Nava Kerala`.
2. Eradicate absolute poverty by uplifting `5 lakh` families.
3. Increase social welfare pension from `Rs. 2,000` to `Rs. 3,000` per month.
4. Assure job opportunities for youth educated in Kerala through campus placements.
5. Launch a `Back to Campus` scheme for skill enhancement.
6. Ensure `50%` women's workforce participation.
7. Create jobs for `20 lakh` homemakers.

### Health, Housing, And Connectivity

1. Offer universal health coverage with effectively unlimited treatment benefits.
2. Create a unified digital health record for all Kerala citizens.
3. Build a lifestyle-disease-free Kerala through preventive healthcare.
4. Continue `Life Mission 2.0` toward a no-homeless State.
5. Build rapid transit systems in `Thiruvananthapuram` and `Kozhikode`.
6. Expand the Water Metro model beyond Kochi.
7. Complete the `Sabarimala Railway`.

## UDF Promise Themes

### Welfare, Women, And Youth

1. Provide `Rs. 1,000` per month for college-going girls.
2. Raise all welfare pensions to `Rs. 3,000` per month.
3. Provide interest-free loans up to `Rs. 5 lakh` for young entrepreneurs.
4. Create a dedicated department for senior citizens' welfare and protection.
5. Provide free bus travel for women in `KSRTC` buses.
6. Fix a minimum daily wage of `Rs. 700` for `ASHA` workers.

### Health, Housing, And Infrastructure

1. Launch the `Oommen Chandy Health Insurance` scheme with `Rs. 25 lakh` coverage per household.
2. Open `Indira Canteens` for subsidized food access.
3. Build `She Hospitals` and related women-focused health infrastructure.
4. Build `5 lakh` houses in five years for the homeless.
5. Launch `Mission Samudra` to integrate Kerala's coastline and maritime connectivity.
6. Expand metro or light-metro connectivity in `Thiruvananthapuram` and `Kozhikode`.
7. Guarantee a minimum support price of `Rs. 300` for rubber.

## NDA Promise Themes

### Welfare And Household Relief

1. Issue a `Bhakshya-Arogya Suraksha Card` with `Rs. 2,500` monthly support for women from poor and BPL households.
2. Raise social welfare pension to `Rs. 3,000` per month.
3. Provide `20,000` litres of free water per household per month.
4. Provide two free LPG cylinders every year during `Onam` and `Christmas`.

### Health And Connectivity

1. Establish an `AIIMS` in Kerala.
2. Develop `Kozhikode` as a medical hub.
3. Build a high-speed rail network from `Thiruvananthapuram` to `Kannur`.
4. Expand aviation connectivity in the State.

## What Happened In The Election

The promises above were released before polling on `9 April 2026`.

The final result was:

- UDF: `102` seats
- LDF: `35` seats
- NDA: `3` seats

This distinction matters because the site should preserve both the pre-election promise record and the post-election outcome.

## How Track Promises Would Store This

Each numbered point above should become a separate promise record tied to the relevant front.

Example fields:

- Tenant: `kerala`
- Timeline: `2026`
- Alliance: `UDF`, `LDF`, or `NDA`
- Election: `Kerala Assembly Election 2026`
- Category: `welfare`, `jobs`, `health`, `housing`, `transport`, or `infrastructure`
- Promise title: short summary of the pledge
- Promise description: fuller wording from the manifesto or campaign article
- Source: URL, quote, publication date, and verification status
- Status: `planned`, `in_progress`, `fulfilled`, `delayed`, or `disputed`
