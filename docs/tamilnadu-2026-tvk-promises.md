# Tamil Nadu 2026 TVK Promises

This note is an example of the kind of public manifesto material Track Promises is meant to capture.

A party or alliance makes a set of promises to voters. We then track each promise as its own record with links back to the election context and original source.

For this example, the tracking context would be:

- Tenant: Tamil Nadu
- Timeline: 2026 Assembly election
- Party or alliance: Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK)
- Source type: manifesto or public campaign promise document
- Tracking unit: one promise per record

## Why This Matters

This is the real shape of the problem the product is solving.

- A party or alliance publishes many promises at once.
- Those promises are grouped under one election and one political identity.
- Each promise should later be reviewed individually for source quality, status changes, and public sentiment.

## Promise Groups

### Women's Welfare And Empowerment

1. Provide `Rs. 2,500` per month in assistance for women heads of households up to 60 years of age.
2. Provide six free LPG cylinders per year.
3. Provide marriage assistance including `8g` of gold and a silk saree for brides from low-income families.
4. Create a dedicated `Rani Velu Nachiyar Force` for women's safety.
5. Provide interest-free loans up to `Rs. 5 lakh` for women-run self-help groups.

### Youth, Employment, And Education

1. Create `5 lakh` new government jobs.
2. Create `5 lakh` stipendiary internships with monthly support of `Rs. 10,000` to `Rs. 18,000`.
3. Provide unemployment assistance of `Rs. 4,000` per month for unemployed graduates.
4. Provide unemployment assistance of `Rs. 2,500` per month for diploma holders.
5. Provide collateral-free education loans up to `Rs. 20 lakh` from Class 12 through PhD.
6. Launch `Vetri Thozhil Munaivor Thittam` with collateral-free loans up to `Rs. 25 lakh` for young entrepreneurs.
7. Introduce a policy reserving `75%` of private-sector jobs for local Tamils.

### Farmers And Agriculture

1. Waive full cooperative crop loans for farmers owning up to 5 acres.
2. Waive `50%` of cooperative crop loans for farmers owning more than 5 acres.
3. Guarantee a minimum support price of `Rs. 3,500` per quintal for paddy.
4. Guarantee a minimum support price of `Rs. 4,500` per quintal for sugarcane.
5. Provide direct aid of `Rs. 10,000` for tenant farmers and agricultural labourers.
6. Create a dedicated department for organic farming.

### Economic And General Welfare

1. Provide `200` units of free electricity per household.
2. Increase monthly pensions to `Rs. 3,000` for senior citizens, widows, and persons with disabilities.
3. Provide health insurance coverage of up to `Rs. 25 lakh` per family.
4. Provide `100%` electricity tax exemption for 5 years for MSMEs.
5. Provide a `35%` capital subsidy for industry support.

### Governance And Infrastructure

1. Commit to a clean, transparent, and corruption-free government.
2. Establish an AI Ministry.
3. Establish an AI City.
4. Establish an AI University.
5. Restore the Cooum and Adyar rivers and take up related urban development projects.

## How Track Promises Would Store This

Each numbered point above should become a separate promise record.

Example fields for each tracked record:

- Tenant: `tamilnadu`
- Timeline: `2026`
- Party or alliance: `TVK`
- Election: `Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2026`
- Category: `Women's Welfare`, `Jobs`, `Agriculture`, `Welfare`, `Governance`, or `Infrastructure`
- Promise title: short summary of the promise
- Promise description: fuller wording from the manifesto
- Source: manifesto URL, quote, publication date, and verification status
- Status: `planned`, `in_progress`, `fulfilled`, `delayed`, or `disputed`
